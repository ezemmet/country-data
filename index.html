ABOUT

The Country Data Web App, created by Nitin Sharma, is a comprehensive tool designed to provide quick and accurate information about any country in the world. With just a search, users can access essential details about a country's capital, currency, population, official languages, borders, and the continent it belongs to. Whether you're a student, traveler, or someone curious about global facts, this app offers a user-friendly interface and a reliable source of information to explore the world at your fingertips. Nitin's passion for data and global knowledge is reflected in this app, which strives to make learning about countries both easy and enjoyable.
